Gyoza

Description

Gyoza are Japanese pan-fried dumplings filled with meat and vegetables. They are crispy on the bottom and juicy inside.

These dumplings are often served as a side dish or appetizer with a simple dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • Gyoza wrappers
  • 200g ground chicken or pork
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup cabbage (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix filling ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Place filling in wrappers and seal edges.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and place dumplings flat side down.
  4. Add a splash of water and cover to steam.
  5. Cook until bottoms are golden and crispy.
  6. Serve with soy sauce.
