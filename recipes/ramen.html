Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup known for its rich broth, chewy noodles, and flavorful toppings. It is one of the most iconic comfort foods in Japan.

This simple version is perfect for beginners and can be customized with different toppings and proteins.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • Green onions
  • Sliced chicken or tofu

Steps

  1. Heat broth with garlic and soy sauce.
  2. Cook ramen noodles separately.
  3. Add sesame oil to broth.
  4. Place noodles in a bowl.
  5. Pour hot broth over noodles.
  6. Add boiled eggs and toppings.
